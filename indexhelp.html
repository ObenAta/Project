How to Use the Homepage

The homepage (index.php) displays general site information, along with a popup message that appears the moment you arrive on the page. This popup provides an overview of key features and functionalities offered by our e‑commerce application. Click I read and understand to close it.

The main content area lists products pulled from the database, ensuring you have a quick view of what’s available. You can also navigate to various site features (including membership details, order history, RMA requests, and more) by using the header links.

Updating Images & Videos:
In order to update images and videos used sitewide, two folders are used: images and videos. For videos, you can manually drag and drop a video file from your computer’s downloads folder into the videos folder. For images, they are automatically added to the website when you add a product. To add or edit a product, you must be in the admin portal, where you will be asked to attach an image.

If you have any additional questions about using the homepage or updating site content, feel free to contact our support team or visit our other help pages for more detailed instructions.
