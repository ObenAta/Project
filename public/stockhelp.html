How to Read the Stock Levels Chart

The Product Stock Levels page displays a bar chart showing real-time stock quantities for each product in your catalog. The chart is generated by Chart.js, ensuring a dynamic, easy-to-read representation of your current inventory.

Hereâ€™s what you need to know:

  • Product Names appear along the horizontal (X) axis.
  • Stock Level values are shown on the vertical (Y) axis. If a product has no recorded stock, its value defaults to 0.
  • You can hover over each bar to see the exact quantity for that product.
  • As your data updates in the database, simply refresh the page to see updated stock levels in real time.

This page is particularly helpful for quickly identifying low or out-of-stock items, and may also highlight which products need restocking.
