How to View Your Order History

On your Order History page, you can track all orders associated with your account. This page is available only once you’re logged in. If you haven’t placed any orders yet, you’ll see a simple message indicating there are no orders on record.

Each entry provides:

  • Product Name – The item(s) you purchased.
  • Order Date & Time – When you placed the order.
  • Order Status – Displays whether your product is Pending or Delivered, based on the expected delivery date.
  • Expected Delivery Date – An estimate based on the order date plus the number of days needed for shipping.
  • Review – A link to leave feedback or a review once an order is placed.

Use these details to keep track of where your purchases stand in the delivery process. If the expected delivery date has passed, the system automatically flags it as “Delivered.”

To leave a review, click the “Leave Review” button, which takes you to our reviews page, pre-filled with your order details.

If you have any questions or concerns about a specific order, please contact our support team for further assistance.
